# Preparing a Menu That Appeals to All Ages

When inviting guests to a banquet or wedding, people often worry about what kind of menu will suit everyone's taste. It is quite challenging when there are guests of various ages in the same event.

## Flavors Familiar to Elders
Most elderly relatives prefer warm soups that are mild in flavor and easy to chew. We prepare these dishes while maintaining the original, traditional flavors they have known and loved for years.

## Preferences of the Youth
On the other hand, younger guests often prefer fried dishes or stir-fries with bold flavors and easy-to-eat textures. We make sure to include these popular choices in our menus as well.

## Balancing the Menu
We avoid preparing dishes that are too difficult to eat or excessively greasy. We help structure a well-balanced menu so that guests of all ages feel comfortable and satisfied after their meal.

### If You're Unsure, Just Let Us Know
Choosing the right menu can be a headache. If you don't know what dishes to select yet, you are always welcome to sit down, have some tea, and chat with us about your needs.
